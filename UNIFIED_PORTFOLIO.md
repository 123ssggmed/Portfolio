## 04 — Pricing Strategy Memo for B2B Workflow SaaS

**Type:** Self-initiated decision-grade memo  
**Area:** Pricing Strategy | SaaS Monetization | Packaging

A pricing strategy memo for a B2B workflow SaaS product moving from a single-tier model to a clearer three-tier packaging structure.

### Recommended Architecture

- Starter: USD 49/month
- Professional: USD 149/month
- Business: USD 399/month

### Strategic Logic

The legacy single-tier model compressed different customer segments into one offer. A tiered architecture creates a clearer upgrade path, aligns features with willingness to pay, and improves monetization without requiring product repositioning.

### Expected Impact Model

- 35-45% projected ARPU uplift
- Churn reduction through better customer-fit alignment
- Expansion revenue upside through governance and collaboration features
- Clearer enterprise sales conversation

### What this demonstrates

Pricing analysis, value-driver logic, segmentation, packaging strategy, SaaS monetization, and executive memo writing.
